Questions

Everything you're wondering.

What exactly is Nimo?
Nimo is an AI-powered companion robot with an expressive face. He learns your routines, reacts to your day, and keeps you company — especially on the road, right from your dashboard.
Does Nimo need WiFi?
Nimo connects over WiFi or your phone to fetch weather, traffic, and messages and to sync his personality. Many of his expressions and interactions work offline too.
Is it safe to use while driving?
Yes. Nimo is designed to be glanceable and hands-free — his expressive eyes and gentle voice keep your attention on the road, not on a screen.
How do I interact with him?
Talk to him, tap the touch-sensitive spots on his head and cheeks, or use the app. The more you interact, the more his personality grows.
